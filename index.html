About Me!

My name is Vivianne Martell... but everyone calls me Vivi! I'm a 21 year old student studying Journalism and Media managemen. Growing up in Miami, fashion has always been part of my life.

Why Do I Love Fashion?

Fashion is and always will be a part of my life. From a young age, I would spend my time playing dress-up, a phase I never seemed to grow out of; I love sharing tips, hacks, and even clothes with those I love. A person's fashion sense will tell you a lot about someone and their unique personality.
