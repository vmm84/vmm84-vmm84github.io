Let's Be Honest...

Fashion is all about expressing yourself! There truly is no right from wrong. However, everyone has their hot takes on trends, and here are some tips to ensure you are always looking sexy and chic.

Wear it Black.

Love or hate it; an all-black fit is always the way to go. Whether you want to play it safe or add a mysterious vibe to yourself, an all-black fit always seems to do the trick!

The Power in a Statement Piece

Recognize the power of accessories! This is one of my favorite tips to share because the perfect accessory can make an outfit go from blah to BAM! Do you have a boring outfit that makes you feel invisible? Well, with the help of a statement bag, you just became the talk of the party.

Confidence is Your Best Friend!

CONFIDENCE IS KEY! This may be the most important one yet. If I had a dollar for every time I went out in that skirt that was way too tight or those jeans from three seasons ago and immediately regretted my decision; I might be able to buy that vintage Chanel bag I have wanted since I was four! The point is, making sure you feel your best and most confident in your outfit is the biggest tip I could ever give. You truly do look your best when you feel your best
